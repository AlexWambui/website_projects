About Me

I am an artist operating under Kenman Creative Creations who mainly deals with interor decoration and liturgical artworks.

From 1987 to 1990 I was attached to Pat Kirby, a leading British Art Illustrator /Visualizer who trained me in illustrations, visualizing and communication in art.

In addition, I trained in Graphic Design and Communication (1988 to 1989) and Basic Draughtsmanship (2000) at the Kenya Polytechnic, Nairobi.

Services

Interior Decoration

e.g. sand blasting, stain glass, artistic wall painting (Murals), landscaping/gardening, sculptures in wood, metal and fiber glass.

Liturgical Artworks

e.g. carved doors, Altar tables, Lectans, the Tabernacles, the Crucifix, Our Lady, statues, grottos and Stations of the Cross.
